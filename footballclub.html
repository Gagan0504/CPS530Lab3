Welcome to GT Soccer Club

GT Soccer Club is a newly opened soccer club in Brampton. This club strives to promote and improve the way soccer is experienced for its members in the city of Brampton. We aim to maintain and increase interest in the sport while fostering physical, mental, social, and leadership skills in our members.
